About Me

I grew up on an avocado ranch in Southern California and moved to Boise to attend college. I will graduate from Boise State University in December, where I will receive a B.S. in Computer Science, a minor in Business and a Leadership and Human Relations Certificate. During my time in college, I found my passion for developing software through finding solutions to community problems.

I have had professional experiences in technical support, software solutions engineering, and Human-Computer Interaction Research. These experiences have challenged me in various fields of Computer Science and have peaked my interst in using mobile and web technologies to help people.

On a more personal level, I am a fairly active guy who enjoys bike rides, camping trips and hosting game nights with friends. I am also very interested in leadership and self-development.

If you'd like to know more about me, don't hesistate to reach out. I'm friendly!
